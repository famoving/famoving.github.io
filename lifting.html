Lifting Techniques

We understand that everyone cannot afford, or simply do not need assistance with their move. Nevertheless, here are a few pointers when lifting items to keep you from harming yourself or others helping you:

  1. Get close to the object
  2. Position feet shoulder width apart
  3. Bend at the knees
  4. Test item for weight
  5. Grasp opposite corners
  6. Lift with a smooth, steady motion
  7. Step and pivot, ensuring not to twist your body

Once you are in full control of the item:

  1. Look before you step
  2. Establish firm footing
  3. Adjust to changing circumstances
  4. DO NOT run, walk briskly at most
