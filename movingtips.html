Moving Tips

Here are a few pointers for moving day:

  • Sell unwanted furniture
  • run washer on spin cycle to rid excess water
  • Use sandwich/ziploc bag to hold screws from disassembled furniture
  • Use color code system to show where boxes to going to go in new house
  • Utlize suitcases and baskets
  • Check with local businesses to obtain free boxes
