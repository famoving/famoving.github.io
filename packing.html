Packing Tips

The most frustrating part of moving is packing. It is tedious, time-consuming, and expensive at times. While we are more than happy to take on this headache for you, here are some tips in case you decide to tackle this giant yourself:

Small Boxes

  • Best used for small fragile or heavy items
  • Examples include canned goods, CD/DVDs, china/dishware, books, electronics, and small miscellaneous items

Medium Boxes

  • Best used for bulky, heavier items
  • Examples include small appliances, computer towers, clothes, shoes, lamp shades

Large Boxes

  • Best used for light or bulky items
  • Be sure to fill this box while monitoring its weight
  • Make sure you can lift the box before closing
  • Examples include linens, lamps, toys...
